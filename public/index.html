About

Hi there,

I am a Web Developer. I'm primarily into problem solving and algorithms. I'm equally interested in UI/UX and have created web components and wire frame designs for clients using Figma.

A strong believer in the power of the internet, I am always up for contributing and ideating towards building new products that solve real world problems.

I'm working towards improving my skills in the realms of web development and problem solving. Looking to collaborate on fun and meaningful projects.

If you're someone with an innovative idea but do not know how to engineer a product(business) out of it, I may be able to help.

Let's connect. Let's do some crazy things together. I'd love to talk business/ discuss ideas over a cup of coffee.
